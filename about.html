About Me

Hello, I'm Kishan Ved! I am a Computer Science Engineering student from the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar. From a young age, I have always been fascinated by technology and its endless possibilities. As I grew older, this fascination turned into a passion for learning about all things related to technology. I am a true learning enthusiast who is always eager to explore new topics and acquire new skills. During my time at Thakur International School, I discovered my love for computers and technology. This led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science Engineering at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar. During my time at university, I had the opportunity to learn from some of the best professors in the field and work on cutting-edge projects that further fueled my passion for technology. Apart from academics, I have also been actively involved in various extracurricular activities such as coding competitions, hackathons, and volunteer work. These experiences have taught me invaluable skills such as teamwork, problem-solving, and time management.
